Frequently Asked Questions - Driving Tips and Test Preparation

Get answers to common questions about driving lessons, test preparation, and safe driving tips.

Book a Lesson

1. What should I bring to my first driving lesson?

You should bring your learner permit, any relevant documents, and wear comfortable clothing and shoes. Check the RSA Driving Test Information for more details.

2. How can I prepare for my driving test?

Practice makes perfect! Schedule pre-test lessons, review the Rules of the Road, and practice mock tests. The RSA Learner Driver Portal is a great resource for tips and guides.

3. What is the EDT program, and is it mandatory?

Essential Driver Training (EDT) is a mandatory 12-lesson program that helps learners develop crucial driving skills. More details are available at the RSA EDT Information

4. How long does it take to learn to drive?

The time varies for each individual. Completing the EDT is required, but extra lessons may be needed to feel confident. Regular practice and support from a driving instructor can accelerate progress.

5. What should I do on the day of my driving test?

Stay calm, arrive early, and bring all required documents. Avoid rushing through any part of the test. Review guidelines on the official RSA site.

6. Are there resources to study driving theory?

Yes! The official Theory Test Ireland site provides practice questions and information on theory tests in Ireland.
